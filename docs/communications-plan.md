## 1. Purpose

This Communication Plan defines how, when, and where project information will be shared among team members during the Bookstore App QA project.  

It ensures that everyone:
- Has access to the same, accurate, and timely information.  
- Understands their responsibilities for reporting progress, risks, and blockers.  
- Maintains clear communication for effective collaboration and decision-making.  

---

## 2. Communication Objectives

1. Promote transparency and accountability across all QA activities.  
2. Provide clear reporting channels for defects, risks, and progress.  
3. Ensure quick feedback loops between QA, developers, and the project lead.  
4. Support remote collaboration using shared tools (Jira, GitHub, and chat).

## 4. Communication Schedule

| Type | Participants | Frequency | Format | Purpose |
|------|--------------|------------|---------|----------|
| *Daily Check-In | All members | Daily | Jira comments or WhatsApp | Status updates and blockers |
| *Weekly Review Meeting* | Entire QA Team | Every Friday | Virtual (Zoom/Meet) | Review progress and plan next week |
| *Mid-Week Stand-Up* | QA Lead + Repo Owner | Wednesday | Chat / Voice | Review build or test environment issues |
| *End-Week Report* | QA Lead → Supervisor | Every Sunday | Email + PDF | Summary of QA progress and findings |
| *Urgent Alerts* | Any member | As needed | Chat / Jira @mention | Critical defects, blockers, or downtime |
